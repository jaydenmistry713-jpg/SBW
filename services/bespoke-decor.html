Décor & Design

Your Vision, Beautifully Realised

At SBW Events, we believe décor is more than decoration — it's the atmosphere, the emotion, and the memory your guests will carry with them. Every design we create is rooted in your personal story, cultural traditions, and aesthetic vision.

From an intimate Mehndi gathering to a grand wedding hall transformation, our décor team works to a custom brief for every event. We combine artisan craftsmanship with an eye for detail to deliver spaces that feel truly extraordinary.

What We Offer

What's Included

A full bespoke décor service, designed around your event from concept through to installation and removal.

  • ✦

    Custom Theme Design

    A full design consultation to establish your colour palette, theme, and mood — ensuring every décor element tells a cohesive, personal story.

  • ✦

    Floral Arrangements & Centrepieces

    Bespoke fresh or silk floral arrangements tailored to your colour scheme, from table centrepieces and aisle florals to bridal bouquets and ceremony garlands.

  • ✦

    Mandap & Stage Décor

    Stunning mandap and stage installations that form the centrepiece of your ceremony — designed with cultural sensitivity and breathtaking visual impact.

  • ✦

    Table Linen & Chair Covers

    Premium linen, chair covers, sashes, and table runners in your chosen colours, giving your reception space a polished, finished look throughout.

  • ✦

    Fairy Light & Draping Installation

    Ceiling draping, fairy light canopies, and ambient lighting installations that transform any venue into a warm, magical celebration space.

  • ✦

    Photo Backdrops & Entrance Décor

    Bespoke photo-ready backdrops, floral arches, and entrance installations that make a statement arrival and create stunning keepsake photographs.
