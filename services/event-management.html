On-The-Day Service

Your Day, Perfected

You've spent months planning every detail. Our event management service ensures all of that hard work pays off — with a dedicated on-site team overseeing every moment of your day so you can be fully present.

From co-ordinating supplier arrivals and managing your full day schedule, to welcoming guests and quietly resolving any last-minute issues, we work discreetly behind the scenes. You and your family enjoy the day; we handle everything else.

Service Breakdown

What's Included

A complete on-the-day service designed to give you complete peace of mind from the first moment to the last.

  • ☑

    Dedicated On-Site Event Manager

    A named SBW Events manager present throughout your entire event, acting as the single point of contact for all suppliers and family members.

  • ☑

    Full Day Schedule Management

    We run your event to a precise timeline, managing transitions between ceremony, reception, and dining so every moment flows seamlessly.

  • ☑

    Supplier & Vendor Check-In

    We meet and brief all suppliers on arrival, confirm setup requirements, and ensure every vendor is in the right place at the right time.

  • ☑

    Guest Management & Ushering

    Welcoming guests, directing them to the correct areas, managing seating, and ensuring VIP guests receive the attention they deserve.

  • ☑

    Problem-Solving & Contingency

    Calm, experienced handling of any unexpected issues — from supplier delays to layout changes — before they ever reach you or your family.

  • ☑

    Post-Event Close-Down

    Overseeing the end of your event, managing supplier departures, collecting hired items, and ensuring the venue is left as agreed.

Our on-the-day event management service suits occasions of every scale across Scotland and North England.
